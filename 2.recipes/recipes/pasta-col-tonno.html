Pasta col tonno

Description

An easy to make pasta dish with delicious tuna.

Ingredients

  • Pasta
  • A can of tuna
  • Tomato Sauce
  • Onions
  • Parmigiano
  • Olive oil

Steps

  1. Add some olive oil and heat it up on a pan.
  2. Add chopped onion and stir occasionally until the onion is golden.
  3. Add tomato sauce and let it simmer for atleast 10 minutes.
  4. Add pasta to boiling water and let it cook for the indicated time.
