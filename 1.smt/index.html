Shin Megami Tensei V

First Map: Da'at

Tutorial Battle

In the first battle you will fight a daemon.

Do not forget to use Zio to strike its weakness and win.

Catch some demons

Use the Talk command to engage in conversation with the demons and make them your allies.

First Boss: Hydra

The first boss is the Hydra from Herculean myth. Its weakness is Ice so hit it with Bufu spells.

If you're having trouble surving after it uses Magatsuhi, you can buy and use Dark dampeners.

Some useful demons:

  • Mermaid
  • Apsaras
  • Neko-shogun for buffs

During "Magatsuhi turns" you should:

  1. Hit with Bufu to gain a press turn.
  2. Hit with Bufu or buff/debuff.
  3. Buff/debuff or hit with Bufu.
  4. Anything as long as two press turns have been obtained.
  5. Anything x4

Second boss: Nuwa

Hit her with Fire and use Force dampeners.

You can get a Rakshasa and the Magatsuhi skill for the Jaki to quickly debuff Nuwa.

Second Map: Da'at 2

First Boss: Loup-Garou

Weak to Bufu but hits like a truck. Use Phys dampeners.

In conclusion

Good game.
